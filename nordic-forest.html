The compute behind the clarity

Heavy computation upstream. Decision-ready intelligence downstream.

Static hazard maps retrieve fixed exposure layers. Fluvion reconstructs an asset’s atmospheric supply chain from large, multi-year climate and Earth-observation datasets. This work requires substantial compute and memory. Moisture is tracked through space and time, reconciled for physical closure, connected to asset geometry, and checked against independent evidence.

The output is a compact, provenance-aware risk object. It can be inspected here and ultimately delivered into portfolio, underwriting, due-diligence, and resilience workflows.

The computational work is centralized. The resulting dependency intelligence moves into the systems where decisions are made.
